**[FAQ Section]**

**H2:** Private Cookie Class FAQs

**H3:** How many people can attend a private cookie decorating class?
Private classes are flexible and can accommodate small groups of 4–6 friends or larger parties of 20+. Share your group size when you request a class and we'll tailor the experience to fit.

**H3:** Do you offer kids' cookie decorating birthday parties?
Yes! Kids' birthday parties and youth group events are some of our most popular private classes. We design age-appropriate themes and provide simple, fun techniques that kids love.

**H3:** What ages are best for a cookie decorating class?
Kids as young as 5 can participate with a parent or guardian's help. Our classes are designed to be fun for all ages — we regularly host mixed groups of kids and adults together.

**H3:** Can you customize the cookie theme for my event?
Absolutely. Sports teams, birthday themes, holiday designs, brand logos, wedding colors, baby shower motifs — you name it. Custom themes are one of our specialties.

**H3:** Do you travel to my location for private classes?
Yes — we offer on-site private cookie decorating classes throughout Folsom, Sacramento, El Dorado Hills, Roseville, Granite Bay, and the surrounding area. We bring all the supplies; you just provide the space and the guests.

**H3:** How long does a private cookie class take?
Most classes run about 1.5 to 2 hours, including practice time and step-by-step instruction on each design. We'll adjust timing based on your group's needs and the number of cookies in your set.

**H3:** What cookie decorating techniques will we learn?
Guests learn royal icing fundamentals including outlining, flooding, wet-on-wet designs, and detail piping. Every class starts with a practice cookie so everyone feels comfortable before decorating their set.

**H3:** How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and holiday seasons. Popular dates fill up quickly, so reach out early to secure your spot.
