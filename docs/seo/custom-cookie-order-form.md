## 1. Executive Summary

### Current State Assessment

The custom cookie order page is the primary commercial conversion page for Rose & Sugar. The current page features:

- A multi-step order request form (name, order details, flavors, vision)
- Pricing callouts ($65/dz starting, 2-week lead time, Folsom pickup)
- Five FAQ entries covering lead time, pricing, dietary restrictions, payment/pickup, and packaging
- Clean, modern design with clear CTAs

**Key Gaps Identified:**

- **Thin body copy**: The page has minimal descriptive content beyond the H1, one paragraph, and the form. Competitors with richer content rank higher for custom cookie queries.
- **No structured data**: No JSON-LD schema markup of any kind (no LocalBusiness, no Product/Service, no FAQPage, no BreadcrumbList).
- **No Open Graph or Twitter Card tags**: Social sharing produces generic previews.
- **Limited occasion-based keyword coverage**: The page doesn't explicitly mention birthdays, baby showers, bridal showers, weddings, graduations, or corporate events in the body copy — all high-intent search terms.
- **No geographic keyword depth**: "Folsom" appears once in a callout box; Sacramento, El Dorado Hills, Roseville, and Granite Bay are absent from the page copy.
- **URL slug is strong**: `/cookies/order-custom-sugar-cookies` aligns well with transactional intent.

### Updated On-Page FAQ Copy

**Q: How far in advance should I order custom cookies?**
A: We recommend placing your custom cookie order at least 2-3 weeks in advance to guarantee your preferred date. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee but cannot be guaranteed, especially during busy holiday seasons. For timing questions, [contact us](/contact).

**Q: How much do custom sugar cookies cost?**
A: Custom decorated sugar cookies from Rose & Sugar start at $65 per dozen and include up to five icing colors with basic to intermediate detail. Character cookies and logo designs start at $70 per dozen. Additional colors, airbrushing, and intricate designs may increase pricing. All orders require a minimum of 2 dozen cookies. [Contact us](/contact) for a personalized quote.

**Q: What areas do you serve for custom cookie pickup?**
A: Rose & Sugar is based in Folsom, CA with pickup available on Saturdays (preferred) or by arrangement. We serve customers throughout the greater Sacramento area including El Dorado Hills, Roseville, Granite Bay, Rocklin, and surrounding communities. We are unable to ship cookies under California Cottage Food laws.

**Q: What flavors are available for custom cookies?**
A: Rose & Sugar offers multiple cookie flavors for custom orders: Vanilla, Lemon, Almond, Confetti, Maple, Chocolate Chip, and a Gluten-Free flour option (available for an additional $6 per dozen). You can also choose dye-free icing for a more natural, muted color palette at $10 per dozen.

**Q: Do you accommodate dietary restrictions for custom cookies?**
A: Our cookies contain wheat, milk, eggs, and soy (in sprinkles). While we cannot guarantee allergen-free cookies, we offer a gluten-free flour option for an additional $6 per dozen and dye-free icing for $10 per dozen. [Contact us](/contact) to discuss your specific dietary needs.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept Venmo, Zelle, and all major credit cards. Payment is required at least two weeks before your scheduled pickup date to confirm your order.

**Q: How are custom cookies packaged?**
A: All custom cookies come individually heat-sealed for freshness at no additional cost. Ribbon-tied packaging is available as an upgrade for an additional charge per dozen. Heat-sealed packaging keeps cookies fresh for an extended period and makes them perfect as party favors or gifts.

**Q: Can I order custom cookies for a corporate event or as client gifts?**
A: Yes! Rose & Sugar creates custom logo cookies, branded cookie sets, and themed designs for corporate events, team celebrations, product launches, and client appreciation gifts. Fill out our [custom order form](#order-form) with your event details and we'll follow up within 48 hours with design ideas and pricing.
