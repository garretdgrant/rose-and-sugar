## 2. Recommended URL Slug

**Primary recommendation:** `/sugar-cookie-recipe`

**Why not `/secret-recipe`:**

- No search volume for "secret recipe" in a cookie context
- Loses all keyword relevance
- "Secret" as a marketing hook works better in the H1 and copy, not the URL

**Alternative slugs considered:**

- `/cut-out-sugar-cookie-recipe` — more specific but longer
- `/best-sugar-cookie-recipe` — competitive keyword but "best" in URLs can look spammy
- `/signature-sugar-cookie-recipe` — brand-aligned but no search volume

**Final URL:** `https://www.roseandsugar.com/sugar-cookie-recipe`

## 10. Payment & Delivery Setup

### Recommended Options (for a Next.js site)

**Simplest:** Use **Stripe Payment Links** — create a payment link for $12, embed the button on the page, and use Stripe's built-in digital delivery to email the PDF after purchase. No additional platform needed.

**Alternative:** Use **Payhip** (free, 5% commission) or **Gumroad** for hosted checkout + delivery if you want a managed platform.

**E-commerce plugin:** If the site already uses a CMS or wants a storefront, consider **Lemon Squeezy** (popular with creators, handles tax automatically).

### Key requirements:

- Instant PDF delivery after purchase
- Email receipt with download link
- No refunds policy clearly stated (standard for digital products)
- Sales tax collection (California requirement — Stripe, Lemon Squeezy, and Payhip handle this)
